Playing Cards Personalities



King of Diamonds

ESFP

King of Diamonds personality type is ESFP, which means that they are extroverted, expressive, and passionate. They are usually extremely outgoing and sociable. They love being in a crowd and in a lively environment. They find a party a real thrill and love to be in the centre of attention. They also have a very infectious sense of fun and enjoy being surrounded by people who have a good time. They have a natural instinct to be in control and will often lead their friends in an effort to keep them safe or entertain them. They love to be the life of the party and enjoy any opportunity to have a laugh and have fun.



Queen of Diamonds

ISFJ

Queen of Diamonds personality type is ISFJ, the Entitled Protector. ISFJ is the most common of all the types, making up 32% of the population, yet it is the one most misunderstood, the most vilified. They are soft-hearted, always ready to help, but can be pushy and stubborn. They are over-protective of others, but can be very controlling and judgemental. They are warm-hearted, caring, but they have a hard time letting go. They are often seen as shy, timid, and reserved, but they can be highly confident and strong-willed. ISFJ has an inflexible need to follow rules and regulations, but they are not stubborn or inflexible about things that are of great importance to them.



Jack of Diamonds

ESTP

Jack of Diamonds personality type is ESTP, described in many detail in the Myers-Briggs type indicator. The head is an open type, with an independent spirit that can be relied upon to take responsibility. The Jack of Diamonds personality type is also described in the Enneagram in the realm of the 10th type, the "Polarizing" type. Jack of Diamonds is one of the polarizing types, and this personality type is described as having an apathetic exterior that hides a powerful and highly developed inner life.

Ace of Spades

INTJ

Intuitive Thinking Judging Personality

This personality type is the most rare of all, and is also the most difficult to identify. INTJ stands for the Intuitive Thinking Judging Personality. People with this personality type are generally well-educated, logical, and capable of seeing multiple sides to any given situation. They tend to be somewhat reserved, but if you get them talking about a topic or a book they’ve read they’re likely to be quite passionate.



Four of Clubs

INTP

INTP Personality Traits

INTPs are often characterized as quiet and introspective by their peers. They are often independent thinkers who have a deep appreciation for the theoretical world, especially the sciences.

INTPs are often more interested in truth than the social world. They are often very intelligent, but they can become bored with their theoretical pursuits.

The INTP personality type is generally self-contained and introspective.
